Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 18, 2026

Aging Gracefully is operated by EnTemps LLC. This policy explains the information involved when you use agingracefully.care.

If you request a listing, correction, or other help, the site opens your email application so you can send the information directly to us. The website does not submit that form data to a separate website database. We receive only what you choose to send by email and use it to respond, evaluate a listing, or correct directory information.

The site does not currently load Google advertising technology. If advertising is enabled in the future, Google and its partners may use cookies or similar technologies to deliver and measure ads, subject to location-specific consent requirements and your choices. Learn how Google uses data on sites that use its services at Google’s partner-sites policy. You can manage advertising preferences at My Ad Center.

Technical information

Our web host and content-delivery providers may process standard request information such as IP address, browser type, requested URL, time, and security logs to deliver and protect the site. We do not sell personal information.

Third-party websites

Provider and resource links lead to independently operated websites. Their privacy practices are governed by their own policies.

Retention and choices

We retain correspondence only as reasonably needed to respond, maintain records, resolve disputes, and meet legal obligations. You may ask us to correct or delete personal information you sent to us, subject to legal and operational retention requirements.

Children

This directory is intended for adults seeking aging-in-place information and is not directed to children under 13.
